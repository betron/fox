i am saying, if you want
to go down
i can take you down
i can take
you out
i can take you under and in
any way that you like
any way that you say
or think
I can count all the steps
to your lower, most tender
parts, i remember the ways
that you want (the all
different ways you would have)
and the different times, different
places you tread
with a slow, slow
slow-dripping delight
my love, i don't need
to imagine, when your skin
is right here, smelling of peach
beneath my nose, i can suck
you in like a breath
of cherries, like a fruit
of the roses
in our small, green garden
where blue and yellow tulips
quietly appear
and watch
